AI Settings

The two optional AI features. Each has its own switch and works with AI off — turn a switch off and that feature falls back to its deterministic result, honestly labelled.

AI features
Master switch. Off = the whole app runs offline: honest deterministic ethics grades and no AI drafting.
AI ethics grading
Grades your ethics highlight semantically with partial-credit tiers and cites the governing Standard. Off = the deterministic exact-phrase grade.
AI tab-to-fill
The ✦ button in the card editor drafts the Back from the Front. Off = the button is inert and no draft is generated.
Your key stays on the desktop. The phone never holds the OpenAI key — it calls your desktop's AI proxy, so AI grading and drafting only reach the model while that proxy is running. When it can't, the feature degrades to its deterministic fallback and says so.
